Awwwards

KPRVerse

KPR excels in collaborative storytelling within the metaverse, blending digital art with real-world experiences. Its art style, inspired by video game illustrations, challenges conventional "anime" aesthetics. The site leverages Web3 technology and fosters an active, diverse community, which has played a key role in its success​.

Webby Awards

What is Missing?

The website What is Missing? is an innovative, multi-platform memorial designed by Maya Lin to raise awareness about environmental loss, focusing on species extinction, habitat degradation, and climate change. It merges art with science through interactive data visualizations, physical installations, and digital experiences. The project’s centerpiece is an interactive globe that users can explore to understand ecological loss and potential solutions to restore biodiversity. It won multiple awards, including the Webby Award for Activism, for its impactful design, navigation structure, and user experience, blending art, technology, and environmental activism seamlessly​
